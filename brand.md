Status: draft-corrected

## Brand Core

Cachin is not just an Argentina QR wallet and not just another crypto wallet.

It is a LATAM payment app for people who arrive with money from outside the local financial system and need to pay like locals.

The brand can be simple and confident, but the underlying proof must stay disciplined. Public-facing claims should map back to the proof files under `docs/proof/` before being used in a hackathon submission, deck, or investor conversation.

Core line:

> Cachin lets tourists and foreigners fund globally, scan supported local QRs across LATAM, confirm the FX, and pay like locals. The MVP starts in Argentina.

Operational line:

> Scan, pay, done.

Origin line:

> Built from LATAM for the world.

Spanish origin line:

> Construido desde LATAM para el mundo.

Sharp product line:

> Cachin lets tourists and foreigners pay like locals across LATAM: fund once, scan supported local QRs, confirm the FX, and pay. The MVP starts in Argentina with Mercado Pago/MODO-style flows.

## Category

LATAM consumer payments / stablecoin-funded wallet / QR merchant network / POS and card-enabled local payments.

## Audience

- Tourists and foreigners traveling across LATAM.
- Digital nomads.
- Freelancers paid in crypto.
- US/EU users who want to fund by SWIFT, IBAN, card, or crypto before traveling.
- Seeker users traveling in LATAM.
- Regular consumers who should not need to understand crypto.
- Local merchants, shops, and supermarkets that want lower fees and faster settlement.

## Positioning

Short:

> Pay like a local in LATAM.

Medium:

> Cachin lets visitors and crypto-paid workers scan local QRs from a funded wallet across LATAM. The MVP starts in Argentina with supported Mercado Pago or MODO-style QRs; onboarded merchants unlock lower fees, instant settlement, discounts, and POS tools.

Full:

> Cachin abstracts crypto into a payment experience people already understand. Users fund the app, scan a local QR, review FX/fees/final total, and pay. Behind the scenes, Cachin uses crypto/stablecoin rails, Sumsub KYC for supported local QR flows, local providers, and Crisp support to make foreign money work inside local payment habits. Onboarded merchants get the enhanced rail: lower fees, instant settlement, discounts, POS tools, and direct confirmation.

## Messaging Pillars

1. **Traveler utility**: Pay like a local even without a local bank account.
2. **Freelancer utility**: Spend crypto income directly without painful cash-out loops.
3. **LATAM QR wedge, Argentina MVP**: Scan supported local QRs across LATAM, starting with Mercado Pago or MODO-style flows in Argentina.
4. **QR-native LATAM behavior**: Cachin fits how the region already pays.
5. **Crypto abstraction**: Users do not need to understand wallets, seed phrases, chains, or rails.
6. **Seeker-ready**: Solana Mobile users can travel through LATAM and pay locally.
7. **Trust layer**: Sumsub KYC for supported local QR flows, pre-payment confirmation, receipt, and Crisp support.
8. **Merchant upgrade**: onboarded merchants unlock better fees, instant settlement, discounts, POS tools, and direct confirmation.
9. **Separate hardware/card line**: Cachin can be the first wallet implementation for a self-custodial cold-wallet/Visa card product, but the card is not the core payment wedge.

## Differentiation Claim

CacaoCash publicly owns a similar "pay QR in LATAM like a local" message. Cachin should differentiate around:

- built from LATAM for the world
- working app status
- scan supported local QRs across LATAM without merchant behavior change, starting with Argentina MVP proof
- Sumsub KYC and Crisp support for user trust
- already onboarded merchants
- merchant discounts
- Cachin POS
- card/cold-wallet payment flow as separate product line / first wallet implementation
- Seeker readiness
- founder local credibility in Argentina/Solana ecosystem
