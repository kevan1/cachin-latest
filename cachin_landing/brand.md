# Brand - Cachin Landing

Status: active

## Core

Cachin is a LATAM-first payment app for travelers, foreigners, digital nomads, and crypto-paid freelancers who need to pay like locals.

Primary line:

> Spend more effortlessly across LATAM.

Operational line:

> Scan, confirm, paid.

Origin line:

> Built from LATAM for the world.

## Voice

Use direct, practical, travel-friendly copy. Avoid DeFi language, speculative token language, and broad coverage claims that are not proven.

## Visual System

- Ink: `#0F1A2A`
- Local sky: `#65D0F9`
- Paid green: `#1FBF7A`
- Offer sun: `#F0A638`
- Paper: `#F6FBFE`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`

Typography uses Fredoka for product voice and JetBrains Mono for labels, amounts, receipts, and rail metadata.

## Claim Guardrails

The landing page should say Argentina MVP and LATAM roadmap unless country-level proof is attached. It should position card/contactless as a complementary product surface, not the core wedge.
